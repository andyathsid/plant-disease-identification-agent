\begin{abstract-id}
    \addcontentsline{toc}{chapter}{ABSTRAK}
    \begin{center}
        {\bfseries \setstretch{\wordonehalfspacing} \MakeUppercase{\thesistitle} \par}
        \vspace{\baselineskip}
        {\setstretch{\wordonehalfspacing} \authorname \\
        \studentid \par}
        \vspace{\baselineskip}
        {\bfseries ABSTRAK}
    \end{center}
    \vspace{-0.5em}
    Penyakit tanaman merupakan ancaman serius bagi ketahanan pangan yang menuntut metode identifikasi cepat dan akurat. Pendekatan \textit{deep learning} konvensional seringkali terbatas pada kelas yang ditentukan dan kurang fleksibel menangani variabilitas di lapangan. Penelitian ini bertujuan mengembangkan sistem agentik untuk identifikasi penyakit tanaman dengan mengintegrasikan deteksi \textit{multimodal} melalui \textit{Vision-Language Model} (VLM) dan kerangka kerja \textit{Retrieval-Augmented Generation} (RAG). Menggunakan metode \textit{Design and Development} (D\&D), sistem dibangun menggunakan ekosistem LangChain dengan mengadopsi pola \textit{Reasoning and Acting} (ReAct) pada agen \textit{Large Language Model} (LLM) dari keluarga model Gemini. Komponen sistem meliputi YOLOv11 untuk deteksi daun, OWLv2 sebagai deteksi \textit{open-vocabulary}, SCOLD yang mengadopsi arsitektur CLIP untuk pengambilan citra-teks penyakit tanaman, mekanisme RAG untuk pengambilan informasi dari basis pengetahuan dan internet, serta antarmuka sistem berbasis web. Hasil menunjukkan YOLOv11s memberikan performa terbaik dengan mAP50 0,89 dan latensi median \qty{21}{ms}. Konfigurasi RAG dengan gemini-embedding-001, BM25, dan rerank-2.5 dari Voyage AI mencapai NDCG@5 0,956, MRR@5 0,959, dan \textit{Recall}@5 0,980. Evaluasi sistem menggunakan \textit{LLM-as-a-judge} pada data distribusi internal menunjukkan rata-rata \textit{Agent Goal Accuracy} 0,96 dan \textit{Disease Accuracy} 0,90, dengan metrik pendukung lainnya di atas 0,90. Pada data luar distribusi, sistem mencapai \textit{Agent Goal Accuracy} 0,84 dan \textit{Disease Accuracy} 0,72, dengan metrik pendukung lainnya di atas 0,89. Pengujian \textit{black-box} menunjukkan seluruh fungsionalitas sistem berjalan sesuai harapan. Integrasi ini menghasilkan sistem identifikasi penyakit tanaman yang fleksibel, adaptif, dan mampu memberikan rekomendasi penanganan yang faktual.

    \vspace{-0.5em}
    Plant diseases pose a serious threat to food security, demanding fast and accurate identification methods. Conventional deep learning approaches are often limited to predefined classes and lack the flexibility to handle field variability. This study aims to develop an agentic system for plant disease identification by integrating multimodal detection through Vision-Language Model (VLM) and a Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) framework. Using the Design and Development (D\&D) method, the system was built on the LangChain ecosystem, adopting the \textit{Reasoning and Acting} (ReAct) pattern in Large Language Model (LLM) agent from the Gemini model family. System components include YOLOv11 for leaf detection, OWLv2 for \textit{open-vocabulary} detection, SCOLD which adopts the CLIP architecture for plant disease image-text retrieval, a RAG mechanism for retrieving information from knowledge bases and the internet, and a web-based system interface. Results show YOLOv11s offers the best performance with an mAP50 of 0.89 and a median latency of \qty{21}{ms}. The RAG configuration with gemini-embedding-001, BM25, and rerank-2.5 from Voyage AI achieved NDCG@5 of 0.956, MRR@5 of 0.959, and \textit{Recall}@5 of 0.980. System evaluation using \textit{LLM-as-a-judge} on in-distribution data showed an average \textit{Agent Goal Accuracy} of 0.96 and \textit{Disease Accuracy} of 0.90, with other supporting metrics above 0.90. On out-of-distribution data, the system achieved an average \textit{Agent Goal Accuracy} of 0.84 and \textit{Disease Accuracy} of 0.72, with supporting metrics above 0.89. Black-box testing confirmed all system functionalities performed as expected. This integration yields a flexible and adaptive plant disease identification system capable of providing factual treatment recommendations.
