\begin{abstract-id}
    \addcontentsline{toc}{chapter}{ABSTRAK}
    \begin{center}
        {\bfseries \MakeUppercase{\thesistitle} \par}
        \vspace{2em}
        \authorname \\
        \studentid \\
        \vspace{2em}
        {\bfseries ABSTRAK}
    \end{center}
    \vspace{-0.5em}
    Penyakit tanaman merupakan ancaman utama bagi produktivitas pertanian global. Keterbatasan metode identifikasi tradisional mendorong kebutuhan akan solusi berbasis teknologi cerdas yang cepat dan akurat. Penelitian ini bertujuan mengembangkan sistem agentik multimodal untuk identifikasi penyakit tanaman dengan mengintegrasikan model bahasa-visual (VLM) dan kerangka kerja \textit{Retrieval-Augmented Generation} (RAG). Metode penelitian yang digunakan adalah \textit{Design and Development Research} (DDR). Sistem dibangun menggunakan ekosistem LangChain dengan mengadopsi pola \textit{Reasoning and Acting} (ReAct) pada agen LLM Gemini 3 Flash. Komponen sistem meliputi YOLOv11 untuk deteksi daun, OWLv2 sebagai deteksi \textit{open-vocabulary}, model SCOLD untuk pencarian gambar-teks penyakit, serta mekanisme RAG untuk pengambilan informasi dari basis pengetahuan dan internet. Hasil penelitian menunjukkan bahwa model YOLOv11 \textit{small} memberikan keseimbangan terbaik dengan mAP50 sebesar \qty{89}{\percent} dan latensi p50 \qty{47}{ms}. Konfigurasi RAG yang menggabungkan \texttt{gemini-embedding-001}, BM25, dan \textit{reranker} Voyage AI mencapai skor nDCG@5, \textit{Recall}@5, dan MRR@5 di atas \qty{90}{\percent}. Evaluasi identifikasi penyakit oleh sistem agentik pada data di dalam distribusi melalui eksperimen tanya jawab visual menghasilkan akurasi berkisar \qtyrange{85}{96}{\percent} dan \qtyrange{60}{79}{\percent} pada data luar distribusi (OOD). Pengujian \textit{black-box} menunjukkan seluruh fungsionalitas antarmuka berjalan sesuai harapan. Integrasi ini menghasilkan sistem identifikasi penyakit tanaman yang fleksibel, adaptif, dan mampu memberikan rekomendasi penanganan berbasis bukti.
    
    \vspace{1em}
    \noindent {\bfseries Kata Kunci:} Penyakit Tanaman, Sistem Agentik, Multimodal, YOLOv11, VLM, RAG
\end{abstract-id}

\begin{abstract-en}
    \addcontentsline{toc}{chapter}{ABSTRACT}
    \begin{center}
        {\bfseries \MakeUppercase{\thesistitleen} \par}
        \vspace{2em}
        \authorname \\
        \studentid \\
        \vspace{2em}
        {\bfseries ABSTRACT}
    \end{center}
    \vspace{-0.5em}
    Plant diseases pose a major threat to global agricultural productivity. The limitations of traditional identification methods drive the need for fast and accurate intelligent technology-based solutions. This research aims to develop a multimodal agentic system for plant disease identification by integrating Vision-Language Models (VLMs) and the Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) framework. The research methodology employed is Design and Development Research (DDR). The system is built using the LangChain ecosystem, adopting the Reasoning and Acting (ReAct) pattern on a Gemini 3 Flash LLM agent. System components include YOLOv11 for leaf detection, OWLv2 for open-vocabulary detection, the SCOLD model for image-text disease retrieval, and a RAG mechanism for information retrieval from the knowledge base and the internet. Research results indicate that the YOLOv11 small model provides the best balance with an mAP50 of \qty{89}{\percent} and a p50 latency of \qty{47}{ms}. The RAG configuration, combining \texttt{gemini-embedding-001}, BM25, and the Voyage AI reranker, achieved nDCG@5, Recall@5, and MRR@5 scores above \qty{90}{\percent}. Disease identification evaluation by the agentic system on in-distribution data through visual question answering experiments yielded accuracies ranging from \qtyrange{85}{96}{\percent} and \qtyrange{60}{79}{\percent} on out-of-distribution (OOD) data. Black-box testing showed that all interface functionalities operated as expected. This integration results in a flexible and adaptive plant disease identification system capable of providing evidence-based treatment recommendations.
    
    \vspace{1em}
    \noindent {\bfseries Keywords:} Plant Disease, Agentic System, Multimodal, YOLOv11, VLM, RAG
\end{abstract-en}
